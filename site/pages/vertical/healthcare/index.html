Delivering quality patient care is a healthcare providers' first priority; however, the business of healthcare can be challenging and costly and finding the right healthcare solutions for their hospital, practice or extended care facility can also pose great difficulty. Konica Minolta and All Covered, our national Managed IT Services division, can improve the health of their organization's productivity with an array of products, solutions and services configured for their budget to solve their unique challenges, so they can concentrate on providing the highest level of patient care.

Our specialized healthcare teams deliver world-class and award-winning MFPs and integrated document management solutions, managed IT and cloud services, Intelligent Information Management solutions, VoIP, video security solutions, and data security and compliance consulting to ensure HIPAA readiness and ongoing compliance with the growing state and national healthcare regulatory requirements.

Dispatcher Phoenix, Dispatcher Stratus and Dispatcher Paragon offers everything from scan capture, to document automation and secure print release solving many workflow challenges.  Paragon includes Epic EHR connectivity for secure print release in larger hospitals or clinics, and Dispatcher Phoenix provides advanced workflow automation assisting customers dealing with time-consuming and costly manual processes as previously mentioned.  Stratus is a cloud-based workflow automation option for customers preferring to minimize or eliminate on-prim servers.  It includes much of the capability of Dispatcher Phoenix, and also offers electronic forms, which can help smaller healthcare organizations more effectively manage their patient intake processes at pre-registration and check-in.Key features for hospitals, clinics, and other healthcare customers include:

  • Upload to Content Management Systems. Dispatcher Phoenix allows you to index, scan and store your paper-based patient records, charts, lab reports, claims and other documents to OnBase by Highland, one of the leading enterprise content management systems used by the healthcare industry today.
  • Print Secure Prescriptions. Consistent with tamper resistant security regulations mandated by the Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services, the Rx Shield process applies anti-copy, anti-erasure and pharmacy alerts to prescriptions printed on plain paper, eliminating the need for costly pre-printed prescription forms.
  • Maintain Security Standards. To assist with HIPAA compliance, Dispatcher Phoenix provides support for HID authentication, audit trails to identify where documents are going, and file conversion to password-protected PDFs. And, to ensure the confidentiality of patient information, Dispatcher Phoenix Healthcare includes an intelligent redaction process that permanently removes all Personally Identifiable Information (PII) and Protected Health Information (PHI) from medical records.
  • Integrated EHR Scanning (HL7 & CDA Compatible). With Dispatcher Phoenix's HL7 Connectivity, staff can securely scan patient information from a Konica Minolta MFP and easily attach that information to the patient record in an EHR. In addition, with Dispatcher Phoenix's CDA Generator, healthcare professionals can automatically process medical documents and patient data into standard HL7 CDA documents, allowing the documents to be communicated between major medical providers and systems. Using Dispatcher Phoenix's intuitive interface at the MFP, staff can easily look up patient data, input additional information such as patient name and medical record number, choose the appropriate document type, and send as an HL7 message to your patient's EHR.

While faxing remains a HIPAA-compliant and user-friendly means of sharing health information, the persistent use of physical fax machines poses serious risks to privacy and security. Data security concerns have fueled a growing trend toward adopting cloud-based services in the healthcare industry. Healthcare organizations are increasingly investing in cloud services to enhance security, regulatory compliance, agility, innovation, efficiency, and cost reduction. By replacing traditional with cloud fax, the healthcare industry can mitigate risks associated with physical fax infrastructure and participate in more robust and protected health data exchange by leveling the playing field.

Benefits

  • Streamlined workflow: Cloud faxing eliminates the need for physical printing, mailing.
  • Enhanced security: Cloud-based portals provide a secure location for storing and accessing faxes, ensuring the confidentiality and privacy.
  • Cost savings: Eliminate the need for dedicated phone lines and associated maintenance costs, resulting in significant cost savings.
  • Integration with EHR and emerging technologies: Digitizing fax workflows allows for integrating artificial intelligence (AI) and other emerging technologies. AI-powered systems can automate post-processing tasks, reducing errors and increasing efficiency.

Sharing information between providers, patients, and health plans has never been more important — or more challenging. Despite the implementation of electronic health records, paper continues to play a role in many clinical and administrative processes. Further, scanning documents to a patient's health record can be a complicated and disjointed process, requiring multiple steps with manual, error-prone processes, consuming administrative staff time. Sharing those documents is largely dependent on legacy fax machines, which pose potential security risks.

The Konica Minolta Healthcare-Enabled MFP Plus, driven by the bizhub One i-Series intuitive interface with Kno2 Connected™ integration, enables healthcare providers to quickly and securely capture, store, and share patient information across the care continuum. With the Healthcare MFP, customers are able to enhance care coordination, reduce costs, maximize staff productivity, and most importantly, improve patient care via the following capabilities:

  • EHR-integrated scanning to a variety of EHR systems, with particular emphasis on Extended Care
  • Direct Messaging for PHI Exchange
  • HIPAA-Compliant Cloud Fax for PHI Exchange*
  • Non-Patient Related Cloud Fax*

*Kno2 also provides a HIPAA-compliant, patient-centric cloud fax service, allowing users to fax documents from their desktop via API integration into applicable EHRs, as well as from the bizhub MFP via the connector app.

Benefits

  • Improves timeliness, accuracy, security and "structure" of vital patient information used within any healthcare community.
  • Drives improved processes during patient / resident intake by providing a more streamlined ability to attach documents to the EHR.
  • Supports Direct Messages by intelligently automating the receipt and delivery of messages with attachments across a secure point-to-point Direct-compliant network.
  • Enables faster, more accurate transfers of care, referrals and other record-sharing activities with outside providers and partners by sending and receiving Direct Messages, as well as HIPAA-Compliant Cloud Fax options.
