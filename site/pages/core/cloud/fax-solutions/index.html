Upland InterFAX is a cloud-based faxing solution. InterFAX is seamlessly integrated into Konica Minolta’s bizhub One i-Series multi-function printers (MFPs), empowering customers to send and receive faxes securely and efficiently from any location without the need for traditional fax infrastructure. The Konica Minolta bizhub One i-Series MFPs, equipped with the InterFAX Marketplace App, offer a robust cloud solution that simplifies the faxing process. Whether through the intuitive in-device app or direct integration with Konica Minolta’s top-of-the-line, cloud-enabled fleet, users can securely send and receive faxes while maintaining compliance with the highest data protection standards.

Benefits

  • Seamless Integration: The bizhub One i-Series MFPs allow users to send and receive faxes directly, eliminating the need for fax boards, servers or standalone devices.
  • Enhanced Security: Strict data protection regulations ensure compliance through advanced encryption and secure cloud storage.
  • Cost Efficiency: Flat-rate pricing for inbound and outbound faxes simplifies budgeting for businesses of all sizes.
  • Scalability: Support fax operations at any scale, whether for small businesses or large enterprises, with consistent, high-quality service.

Kno2 is a HIPAA-Compliant clinical document exchange solution built exclusively for healthcare providers with cloud fax, Direct Secure Messaging and integrations into leading electronic health record systems (EHR). With Kno2, providers can achieve nationwide data sharing, even with fax, as they work to modernize their systems over time.

Integrated Fax Workflow
Easily incorporate fax into clinical providers’ interoperability workflows, EHRs and other clinical systems to centralize communications, including Direct Secure Messaging and other healthcare exchange protocols.

API Integration
Easily look up patients from the fax intake screen, with the ability to add structure to unstructured fax documents (e.g., name, DOB, gender) for smoother EHR ingestion.

Simplified Faxing
Send faxes directly from the desktop or through the Konica Minolta bizhub Healthcare-Enabled MFP.
