                  RSVP, and Digital Hall Pass solutions.
                </p>
                <p>
                  The visitor management software, is now available on-premises
                  or as a cloud-hosted solution. This hybrid software as a
                  Service (SAAS) solution gives you the flexibility to host data
                  on your own network or in a secured hosted environment.
                </p>

                  <strong
                    >There are three versions of the software available
                    on-premises or as a hosted solution:</strong
                  >
                </p>

                  roll of the time-expiring visitor badge label stock, 1 sheet

                  a district provided PC. Price includes first year support.
                </p>
                <p>
                  •&nbsp;<strong>School Gate Guardian Self-Serve Bundle</strong
                  >– Includes SGG Self-Serve software, SGG Pro software,

                  single-sided thermal badge printers, unlimited sex offender
                  look-ups, 1 roll of the non time-expiring visitor badge label
                  stock, 1 sheet of the frequent visitor key tags (24 per
                  sheet), installed on a district provided pc. Price includes
                  first year support.
                </p>
                <p>
                  •&nbsp;<strong>School Gate Guardian Self-Serve Kiosk</strong>–
                  Includes the School Gate Guardian Self-Serve software, SGG Pro
                  software, small form factor Lenovo pc, Elographics Acoustic
                  Pulse Recognition touchscreen, AssureTec ID-150 duplex ID
                  scanner, 2 Code Reader omni-directional bar-code readers, 2
                  webcams, 2 thermal badge printers, USB speaker, surge
                  protection, mini-hub, one roll of the non time-expiring
                  visitor badge stock, 1 sheet of frequent visitor key tags (24
                  per sheet), unlimited sex offender look-ups, all enclosed in
                  an attractive kiosk. Price includes first year support.
                </p>
